WeatherWear

Welcome to the website that helps you pick out your outfit of the day based on the weather. Whether it's raining or sunny, we got your back!

Has this ever happened to you?

You walk outside on a beautiful sunny afternoon in shorts and a T-shirt, and you head to a picnic, when all of a sudden it starts to rain! You forgot your rainboots and umbrella, and now you're all wet.

Don't worry, this website has everything you need to look stylish and be prepared for the weather!

Weather Types

Summer

Creme lace shorts are a great staple for every closet. They match everything and add a little spice to your outfit. Pair it with a light-washed light wash jean button-down shirt.It is light and care free.

Add some flare to your tank top with a cute short skirt with funky bright designs. If you're really daring, add high heels! This can make your outfit go from day time to night time with one change.

Spring

A mid sleeve lace dress is perfect for spring. Lace is a light material to you keep you look but covered at the same time. Add a belt for struction and for some color. The light colors of lace are perfectly symbolize that warm weather is coming!

Winter

When it is cold you just want to snuggle up and be warm, but at the same time you want to be fashionable. Have you ever stared at your closet for 15 minutes wondering what to wear? Well now you dont need to have that problem anymore!

To stay warm but fashionable the key is lots of layers. Start off with a simple sweater of a color with your choice. Add a patterened scarf for a pop of color and extra warmth. Add a long trench coat that is sleek annd stylish. To pull the look together add a hat with a textured pattern. The accessories is where you can add your personality. You cna do things like add a bright colored purse or add some shiny jewelry. A classic riding boot will match will be the perfect accompaniment to the outfit.

Fall

Fall is one of the best times of the year! You can pretty much anything from dresses to shorts to skirts. A great fall outfit is filled with color and should be able to accomadate the warm afternoons but cold morningings and nights.

In fal you want to be able to stay warm but cool at the same time. A maxi skirt is the perfect piece of clothing for fall. A light color skirt with a patterned think sweater will do just the trick. The long sleeves and skirt will keep you warm in the morning and night but the clothing is thin enough and flowy to also keep you cool. Arm candy would be a great accessory! Just put together a nice watch and a bunch of different braclets. A statement necklace to match the braclets will pull the whole look together.

Rainy

Everyone is always wondering what to wear when it rains, but do not worry anymore! This website will let you exactly what to wear when it rains so that none of your clothes get ruined.

Everyone gets lazy when it rains and all you want to do is be comfortable. A long loose tunic which leggings is perfect for a rainy day. Functionable and fashionable rain boots such a Hunters are perfect and they come in many different colors to brighten up your look. A knit cardigan is a great pair for the outfit to keep you warm. Don't forget your umbrella!
